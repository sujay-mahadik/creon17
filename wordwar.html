- WORD WAR -

• ENTRY FEE - Rs. 100

• Duet entry

• No of rounds: 2

ROUND 1:

• Topics will be Current Affairs , common and popular

• The topic will be provided to the team 20 min before the debate

• Mobile Phones will be allowed during the debate

• Any Mutually Understandable language will be allowed to use

• Total time: 10 min per Pair of team

i) 1 min Introduction per team

ii) 3 min One on One debate

• Round will be conducted for 2 Hours

ROUND 2:

A)Semifinal

• Topics will be Current Affairs , common and popular

• The topic will be provided to the team 20 min before the debate

• Mobile Phones will be allowed during the debate

• Any Mutually Understandable language will be allowed to use

• Total time: 10 min per Pair of team

i. 1 min Introduction per team

ii. 3 min One on One debate

B)Final

• The Topics will be Less Common yet of Great significance

• The topics will be given 45 min before the debate

• Mobile phones are allowed for this round as well

• Language will be Restricted to using English (as much as possible)

• The Time of Debate per pair of team will be 30 min

o 2 min per team for introduction

o 10 minutes for controlled cross questioning(mediator present)

o 10 min for open debate

o 1 min for the judges to ask questions

o 2 min for the audience to ask questions

o 2 min Conclusion for Each team

• The Second Round will begin at 2 pm

Rules For Open Debate:

• Speaking most and not letting the other team speak will win points

• Smart answers over aggressive replies preferred

• Fighting with topic knowledge is expected

• Clear expression of ideas expected

• Each member should be equally anticipating
