- INSIGNIA -

• ENTRY FEE - Rs. 50

• Cash Prize - Rs. 1000

• Last Date : 09/02/2017

• Submit @ mit.spandan@gmail.com

THEME:Light (SPANDAN Logo)

SPANDAN is the name of MITCOE’s annual college magazine.

The winning entry of this competition may be featured as the logo of the magazine of the academic year 2016-17.

The logo can be symbolic or typographic(SPANDAN’17) or however the designer wishes.

RULES:

• The contest is open for all but through online submissions only, and the entries should be mailed to mit.spandan@gmail.com

• Submissions will not be accepted once the deadline lapses.

• Individuals may submit no more than two entries.

• Logo should be designed with suitability to the college magazine.

• All submitted work must be the original work of the entrant.

• Each entry must be credited to a single individual. MIT CREON will not accept entries from pairs or teams.

• All entries will become the property of MIT CREON & SPANDAN.

• The winning logo will be featured as the magazine logo for the academic year 2016-17 as per the decisions of MIT CREON.

• All entries must be submitted as scalable vectors, and must be adaptable to change, including size alterations and color/grayscale shifts.

• MIT CREON reserves the rights to call on the original work files of the winner.

Caption Information during mailing the entries should include:

1. Name of the participant.

2. City of the participant.

3. .png or .jpeg file along with its vector file (.cdr, .ai, .eps)

4. Description of the logo.
