MIT CREON

MIT CREON is the Literature and Arts Fest of MITCOE, Pune. This event is organized by team SPANDAN, the magazine team of the college. This year MIT CREON is to be held on 10th and 11th of February 2017. CREON was first held in 2012. CREON is a one of a kind event, which is an epitome of the collaboration between arts and literature.

Creativity at its peak, CREON is a unique fest which has something for everyone. MIT CREON 2016 was held on a national level, the theme of MIT CREON 2016 was Around the World, which took the college, along with all of India on a sensational journey through the world! This year, MIT CREON 2017 promises another exciting theme, A Magical realm, which looks forward to bestow a magical experience to all.

ENCHANTIA - A MAGICAL REALM

Every day, in a routine life, so repetitive, we look for magic and excitement to brighten up the moment. Maybe a small smile, a small miracle, something awesome or something even normal manages to make the heart happy, and the mind rejuvenated. This year, at MIT CREON, 2017, we bring two days, for you to live magical life here at MITCOE!

A magical place, with every arcane thing you can ask for.This February, step into a magical realm and relive dreams or fantasies that take you out of the normalcy and give your mind new realms, food for imagination and break from routine!
